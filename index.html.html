What started as a early-stage business quickly turned into a passion for capturing real, unprecedented technology that speaks louder than words.

Born on May 8, 1996, in Amsterdam, Netherlands, Termaaten is of Dutch nationality. He studied at Rotterdam University, where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Finance. Before founding Vertical Studio AI, he launched and successfully exited two ventures - Handz Nu and Silver Design & Development. His leadership at Vertical Studio AI reflects his long-term mission to bridge advanced AI technology with practical, everyday use.

When I’m not behind the camera, you can find me at conferences worldwide and my favorite place- the office.
